# User Manual - Fintech AI Assistant

## Application Overview
Fintech AI Assistant is your personal financial companion. It helps you track where your money goes, suggests ways to save, and uses AI to predict your future expenses.

## User Flow
1. **Splash Screen**: Initial loading of the app.
2. **Login/Signup**: Create an account or log in to secure your data.
3. **Dashboard**: View your total balance, recent transactions, and AI insights.
4. **Add Income/Expense**: Manually enter your financial data.
5. **OCR Scanner**: Scan a receipt to automatically log an expense.
6. **AI Assistant**: Talk or type to the assistant to get financial advice.
7. **Trends & History**: Explore your financial data through charts and lists.

## Screen Descriptions

### Dashboard
The central hub showing:
- Current Balance, Total Income, and Total Expenses.
- Quick access buttons for OCR Scanning and AI Assistant.
- Recent transaction list.
- A "Budget Status" progress bar.

### OCR Scanner
- Tap the "Scan" button on the Dashboard.
- Point your camera at a receipt.
- The app will highlight the date and amount.
- Confirm the details to save the expense.

### AI Assistant
- Tap the AI Assistant button.
- Type a question like "How much did I spend on food this month?"
- The AI will analyze your data and provide an answer.

### Spending Trends
- Navigate to the "Trends" tab.
- View a Pie Chart showing expense distribution by category.
- View a Bar Chart showing monthly spending comparisons.

## Feature Usage Instructions

### Adding an Expense via Voice
1. Long-press the AI Assistant button or tap the microphone icon.
2. Say: "I spent 50 dollars on groceries."
3. The app will parse the amount and category and ask for confirmation.

### Viewing History
1. Tap the "History" tab.
2. Use the search bar to find specific transactions.
3. Tap on any transaction to edit or delete it.
